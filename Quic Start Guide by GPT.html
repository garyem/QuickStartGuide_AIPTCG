🔮 Quick Start Guide

For: New Users of the AI-Powered Training Content Generator
Time to Complete: ~5 minutes

🙋 1. What This App Does

This app helps trainers instantly generate ready-to-deliver training content — such as lesson plans, exercises, quizzes, and more — simply by entering a few key details.

Think of it as your personal AI co-designer for workshops.

⚖️ 2. How to Use it (Step-by-Step)

✅ Step 1: Go to the app
https://aipoweredtcg.onrender.com

✅ Step 2: Fill in the form

  • Topic: What the session is about (e.g. ‘Conflict Resolution’)
  • Duration: Choose how long (e.g. 30 min, half day)
  • Language: Default is English
  • Components: Tick what you want — lesson content, quiz, reading, etc.

✅ Step 3: Click “Generate”
The AI will create content instantly — no need to prompt or write anything yourself.

💡 3. Example

Your Input:

  • Topic: “Customer Service”
  • Duration: Half-day
  • Components: Lesson + Exercises + Quiz

AI Output:

  • ✅ A half-day session with:
  • Key learning points
  • Practical activities
  • Quiz questions with answers

You can copy-paste, edit, or print this for your training delivery.
